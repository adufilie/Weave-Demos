This visualization shows the graduation rate for students at Universities in the Massachusetts State System and the University of Massachusetts System for cohorts of students entering in 1999, 2000, 2001, 2002, 2003, and 2004.


These statistics are broken down by race (black, white), by hispanic ethnicity, and by gender.


Graduation rate is calculated for campuses of the two systems separately as: Number graduating with Bachelor's or equiv subcohort (4-yr institution) Completers within 150% of normal time. These are then summed and expressed as a percentage of total graduating.
